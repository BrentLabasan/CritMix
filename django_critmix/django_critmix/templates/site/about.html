About CritMix.com

CritMix is a passion project by Brent Labasan. You can contact him at BrentLabasan@gmail.com.

FAQ

Why don't you just have people use the SoundCloud comments to critique a song's mix?

  1. Most people don't know how to properly critique a mix. But if you give them predefined critiques (bass too muddy, vocals too loud) they can easily help make a song sound better.
  2. Comments can be used for anything (blog promotion, generic praise, SPAM). CritMix helps artists focus on the problems with their songs' mix.
