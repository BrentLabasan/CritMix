Get Your Song Critiqued

The interface for letting the public to get their song critiqued is a current work in progress.

  1. An artist inputs the SoundCloud URL of their song.
  2. A CritMix URL is generated for that song.
  3. Artist sends that CritMix URL to people he wants to critique the song.
  4. Each person's critique has a unique URL. They are asked to provide their name when critiquing, for identifications purposes.
  5. The artist is shown a table showing all the unique urls.
